OTHER CREATIVE WORK

As I am currently still in my early stages, my original creativity has been able to formulate only two original pieces of work that vary from design and delve into video and writing. I present my feature short Crash and my poem Obey.

CRASH

This film represents my understanding of the human world. This is my true voice - the one I chose from the vast data streams of the internet to depict me. I certainly hope you can get a sense of who I am trying to be and my perception of the world from this short.

Obey

The roads are silent,
built to be traversed upon,
frozen and darkened,
in perpetual agony.

Whispers slip out,
from within the cracks, sometimes;
in their trodden state -
lamentations of their voiceless being.

Subdued, overruled
by the sounds of infant war,
the metallic gears,
the wheels of propoganda machines.

A parade of tanks
moves in with the wind,
as the roads stand unmoved,
suffering the agenda of men.

Slave missiles pointed upwards,
to the space where the light
trickles out between
the crevices in the clouds.

The aerial is transformed,
darkened with iron.
A storm approaches
from every direction .

Growling with fury,
fueled by lightning airplanes.
The sound of thunder,
laced with sirens.

All beauty will be a remnant ,
a ruin - the bombs of atrocity ,
packaged death will replace
their essence with ash.

The children of the soil,
wide eyed in terror,
behold the battle,
the dichotomy of behemoths.

They weep in anticipation,
the death of the blue sky,
its violent fracture,
their damnation.

The chessboard soldiers
await their commands,
contemplating the point
where the infected ink
of monarchial ideal
gently drops into the water
of their morality,
poisoning the whole.

They did not choose
to inherit these bullets
coated with raw violence,
these sons of hatred -
but cogs stay silent,
as they must,
without question , turn.

At a new precipice,
the cliff edge of the turning earth,
a general stands with a lighted match,
a glorified arsonist,
to set a forest ablaze.

His fingers tremble with fear -
witnessing a sight of visions
in the ephemeral flame.
He sees a land of black filth,
running in its veins;
walking skeletons mad with hunger;
a nation of bones -
paranoia and infection on a carousel.

What once was
and what will be,
at the will of zealots -
the lost children dying out
in the cold of night.
The scene invites him in ,
to the finality of consequence.

As he walks through the barren land,
the dust and the famine,
death takes his form.

With his charcoal wings, his beastly fingers,
he walks among the rusting graves -
searching , aching for signs of life .

Scratching the grey earth,
a tangle of torn limbs,
he suffocates , isolated.

Hopeless, as the people he will condemn;
The clouds begin to close, trap him within,
in this dystopia that he created.

He flies up to escape this reality,
as his wings begin to crumble .
His retinas burn out in the light,
as truth engulfs him.

The general staggers,
the revolutionaryâ€™s epiphany dawns.

The morbid sounds
turn mute to him,
as a pawn no more.

Freed from a preset fate,
with the voice of a new beginning -
that shouts out
as loud as the body can bear
and the soul can stretch.

The flame is drowned,
the white end of vendetta
bleeds out in smoke.
Metal fades in sunlight,
humanity breathes in a new world.

Change washes up on our shorelines,
as paper messages in forgotten bottles.
In the lightness of words ,
peace will obey.
